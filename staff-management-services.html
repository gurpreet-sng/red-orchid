Staff Management Services

At Red Orchid Hospitality Pvt Ltd, we pride ourselves on being a trusted name across multiple industries. With a rich portfolio encompassing Project Management, Healthcare, Staffing Solutions, and Real Estate, we bring a unique blend of expertise to every project.

Red Orchid Hospitality Pvt Ltd is a leading provider of skilled manpower across various industries, with a particular focus on the aviation and hospitality sectors. We understand that the success of any business depends on the quality of its workforce, which is why we are committed to supplying only the most qualified and capable personnel to meet our clients' needs.

A. Aviation Industry

The aviation industry demands a high level of expertise and professionalism, and we are proud to supply skilled manpower for various roles within this sector. Our services include the provision of:

  • Ground Staff :From check-in agents to customer service representatives, our ground staff are trained to provide exceptional service and ensure smooth operations at the airport.
  • Flight Attendants :Our flight attendants are trained to the highest standards, ensuring the safety and comfort of passengers on board.
  • Flight Instructors: We provide experienced flight instructors who are capable of training the next generation of pilots and aviation professionals.
  • Loaders/Porters: Our loaders and porters are skilled in handling luggage and cargo efficiently and safely, ensuring that operations run smoothly.
  • Cleaners and Airport Labor: We provide a dedicated team of cleaners and airport labor who are responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and functionality of airport facilities.
  • Cargo Handling: Our cargo handling staff are trained to manage and process cargo efficiently, ensuring that all shipments are handled with care and precision.
  • Complete Airport Hospitality Service: From greeting passengers to managing VIP lounges, our airport hospitality services ensure that every aspect of the passenger experience is taken care of.
  • Aircraft Cleaning: We provide specialized aircraft cleaning services, ensuring that all aircraft are maintained to the highest standards of cleanliness and hygiene.
B. Hospitality Industry

In the hospitality sector, we supply skilled manpower for various roles, including:

  • Duty Managers :Our duty managers are experienced in overseeing hotel operations, ensuring that guests receive the highest level of service.
  • Chefs :We provide skilled chefs who are experts in various cuisines, ensuring that your guests enjoy a memorable dining experience.
  • Housekeeping Staff :Our housekeeping staff are trained to maintain the cleanliness and orderliness of your establishment, ensuring that all areas are spotless and well-maintained.
  • Telecallers :We supply telecallers who are skilled in customer service and sales, helping to drive revenue and enhance guest satisfaction.
  • Service Team :Our service team is dedicated to providing exceptional service in all areas of your establishment, from the front desk to the dining room.
  • Security :We provide trained security personnel who are responsible for maintaining the safety and security of your premises.
  • Engineers and Technicians :Our engineers and technicians are skilled in maintaining and repairing various systems and equipment, ensuring that your operations run smoothly.
